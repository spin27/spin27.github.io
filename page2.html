KIWI (not the fruit)

Kiwi are the only bird that have nostrils at the end of their beaks. Kiwi are mostly noctural and eat insects, grubs, earthworms, fruit, and native plants. Kiwi have an acute sense of smell to help kiwi in the night. Weasels and ferrets are the main predators to kiwi, and suprisingly cats and dogs come next. When kiwi walk, their bills make tapping noises on the ground as they sniff and prod soil. The lifespan of a kiwi is around 25-50 years which is incredible for a bird!
