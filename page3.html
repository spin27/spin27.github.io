About me

I am a junior in High School and am excited for summer! I have played the violin for over 11 years. I also do field hockey and rowing at the high school. My favorite color is neon pink or blue. The one thing I hate most about the great outdoors is ladybugs. I think this fear of ladybugs came from a traumatizing experience with them. My favorite food group is pasta. The best pasta shapes are gemelli, wagon wheels, bucatini, or fettucine (best that I can think of). My favorite animals are pangolins, sloths, llamas, alpacas, koalas, and kiwis.
